Asset Lifecycle

IT Asset Lifecycle Management

From procurement to NIST 800-88 compliant disposal, we manage every stage of your IT assets. Secure data destruction, certified recycling, and full audit trails — so you stay compliant and your data stays safe.

What We Offer

End-to-end asset management.

Procurement & Sourcing

Hardware sourcing, vendor negotiation, and purchase management. We find the right equipment at the right price from trusted suppliers in Japan and globally.

Asset Tracking

Real-time inventory management with detailed reporting. Know where every asset is, who has it, and when it's due for refresh — all from a centralized dashboard.

Data Destruction (NIST 800-88)

Certified data sanitization compliant with NIST 800-88 guidelines. Every drive receives a certificate of destruction with a complete audit trail for your records.

E-waste Recycling

Environmentally responsible disposal through certified recycling partners. We ensure your retired assets are processed in compliance with Japanese environmental regulations.

Our Process

Four stages, fully managed.

01

Procure

We source hardware from trusted vendors, negotiate pricing, and handle purchase orders so you get the right equipment without the procurement headaches.

02

Deploy

Configuration, imaging, and rollout to your team. Every device is set up to your specifications and ready to use on day one.

03

Manage

Ongoing asset tracking, maintenance scheduling, and lifecycle reporting. We monitor warranty status, plan refresh cycles, and keep your inventory accurate.

04

Retire

Secure data destruction with NIST 800-88 compliance, certificates of destruction, and environmentally responsible e-waste recycling through certified partners.
