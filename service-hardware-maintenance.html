What We Offer

Proactive hardware care.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspections, cleaning, firmware updates, and health checks that catch issues before they cause downtime. Extend equipment lifespan and reduce unexpected failures.

Break-Fix Repair

Rapid diagnosis and repair of hardware failures. We stock common replacement parts for servers, workstations, and network equipment to minimize downtime.

Spare Parts Management

Strategic spare inventory based on your critical equipment and failure patterns. Pre-positioned parts for rapid replacement when you need them most.

Lifecycle Reporting

Track equipment age, repair history, and total cost of ownership. Data-driven refresh recommendations when repair costs exceed replacement value.
