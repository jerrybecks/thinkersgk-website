Compliance

Standards We Meet

Full compliance with international and Japanese data protection regulations.

NIST 800-88

Guidelines for Media Sanitization. Our processes follow Clear, Purge, and Destroy methods as defined by the National Institute of Standards and Technology.

DoD 5220.22-M

U.S. Department of Defense standard for data sanitization. Multiple-pass overwrite method ensuring complete data elimination.

APPI (Japan)

Act on the Protection of Personal Information. We ensure compliance with Japan's data protection requirements for proper disposal of personal data.

ISO 27001

Information security management aligned processes. Our data destruction procedures follow ISO 27001 Annex A controls for media handling.
