What We Offer

Complete wireless assessment.

Site Survey & Heat Mapping

We walk your entire floor plan with professional tools to map signal strength, interference sources, and dead zones. Detailed heat maps show exactly where coverage is strong and weak.

Capacity Planning

Analyze current and projected device density to ensure your wireless infrastructure can handle growth. We plan for meeting rooms, hot desks, and BYOD environments.

AP Placement & Configuration

Optimal access point placement based on survey data. We configure channels, power levels, and band steering to maximize throughput and minimize interference.

Post-Deployment Validation

After installation, we re-survey to verify coverage meets targets. Ongoing monitoring ensures your Wi-Fi stays fast and reliable as your environment changes.
