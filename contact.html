FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Tokyo and primarily serve the Kanto region, but we have partners and capabilities throughout Japan, APAC, and globally for multinational clients.

Do you offer 24/7 support?

Yes, we offer 24/7 support for critical issues. Our standard business hours are Monday-Friday, 9AM-6PM JST, but we're always available for emergencies.

Do you provide bilingual support?

Absolutely. Our team includes native Japanese and English speakers, ensuring clear communication regardless of your preferred language.

How quickly can you respond to issues?

Our average response time for critical issues is 15 minutes. For non-critical requests, we typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours.

What industries do you work with?

We work with businesses across various industries including technology, finance, manufacturing, healthcare, and professional services.

We offer both fixed monthly retainer packages and project-based pricing. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.
