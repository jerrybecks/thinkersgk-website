How It Works

From request to resolution.

1

Request

Submit a dispatch request via phone, email, or our ticketing system. Include site details and issue description.

2

Dispatch

We assign the right engineer based on skill set, location, and urgency. You receive confirmation with ETA and technician details.

3

Resolve

Our technician arrives onsite, diagnoses the issue, and performs the repair or installation. Real-time updates throughout.

4

Report

Receive a detailed service report with work performed, parts used, and recommendations for preventing future issues.
